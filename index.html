ABOUT US

Sophia Landscaping & Tree Service LLC

The Mission

Sophia Landscaping's goal is to bring beautiful landscaping to the DMV area. Making outside spaces new areas to live in, hoping to bring families together outside. We are always trying new things and doing whatever we can to make the customers vision come true.

Our Process

• Recieve an estimate in person or virtually.

• Generate detailed contract with the scope of work and pricing.

• Get the job on our schedule.

• Invoicing is done once the customer is satisfied with our work.

Trust The Experts

We take pride in our team's training, skills, and ability to perform professional work by serving the DMV for 15 years. It is important for our customers to be confident in the crew members that they welcome to their properties.
